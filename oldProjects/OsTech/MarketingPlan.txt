OS TECH RESIDENTIAL MARKETING PLAN
=====================================

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS (This Week)
------------------------------

1. GOOGLE BUSINESS PROFILE (FREE)
   - Create/claim: https://business.google.com
   - Add: Photos, service area, hours, services
   - Goal: Get 10+ reviews in first month

2. GOOGLE ADS
   - Setup: https://ads.google.com
   - Budget: $800/month starting
   - Target keywords:
     * "home theater installation near me"
     * "wifi installation [city name]"
     * "smart home wiring Indiana"
     * "ethernet installation residential"
     * "security camera installation"
     * "whole home audio"
   - Expected cost: $3-8 per click

3. TRUCK/VEHICLE BRANDING
   - Add phone number + "Residential Services" to vehicles
   - Cost: $2-4k, lasts 5-7 years


MONTH 1-2 ACTIONS
-----------------

4. FACEBOOK/INSTAGRAM BUSINESS
   - Setup: https://business.facebook.com
   - Budget: $500/month
   - Content: Before/after projects, video walkthroughs
   - Target: Homeowners 35-65 in service area

5. NEXTDOOR
   - Join: https://business.nextdoor.com
   - Post helpful tips, respond to leads
   - Paid ads optional (cheap, effective)

6. STRATEGIC PARTNERSHIPS
   Partner with:
   - Local electricians (referral fee: $100-200/job)
   - Home builders (new construction)
   - Interior designers (high end residential)
   - Real estate agents (home upgrades)


ONGOING ACTIONS
---------------

7. LEAD GENERATION PLATFORMS (Later)
   - Angi: https://www.angi.com (Look for "Join as a Pro" or "For Businesses")
   - Thumbtack: https://www.thumbtack.com/pro
   - Note: Expensive leads ($30-80 each), wait until established

8. REFERRAL PROGRAM
   - Offer: $100 credit for customer referrals
   - Track and pay out promptly

9. SEO / WEBSITE UPDATES
   - Add "Residential Services" section to homepage
   - Create location pages for each city served
   - Blog monthly: smart home tips, project showcases
   - Optimize meta descriptions with residential keywords

----------------
