Our Story

OS Tech was founded in 2023 by Oswaldo Martinez. Since 2015, Oswaldo has worked in low voltage across Indiana, spending the majority of that time as a supervisor leading crews on commercial and institutional projects. Schools, movie theaters, corporate offices, entertainment venues. We've seen it all.

OS Tech was built around a team that has worked together for years. Our goal is simple: deliver quality work with the personal attention that only a tailored, local team can provide.
